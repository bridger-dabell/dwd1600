How Does the Violin Work?

  1. The bow rubs on the strings causing them to vibrate and have sound
  2. The Holes on the violin work together with the bridge (The arch looking thing) to have sound

Instructions

How to hold the violin?
Put your shoulder rest on, hold violin against chin, and put fingers on the fingerboard.
How to hold the bow?
Put hand on bow and stay relaxed, then put thumb bent
